--- layout: post title: Dequindre Cut Extension date: '2012-09-18T09:48:00.002-04:00' author: DA tags: - Detroit - Dequindre Cut modified_time: '2012-09-18T10:01:43.377-04:00' thumbnail: http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-bvshXK8gPLY/UFh7mP07d0I/AAAAAAAABSg/ljD1-x-TuFs/s72-c/11215558-standard.jpeg blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-17447662.post-1881341352981922609 blogger_orig_url: http://daveande.blogspot.com/2012/09/dequindre-cut-extension.html --- Good news for the Dequindre Cut - a plan to extend the pathway through Eastern Market and onto Midtown and Hamtramck will move forward in the City Council and has a $10 million federal grant to help pay for the costs. I don't known anything about real estate development but something tells me that a complex plan like this would have been already executed upon in a more progressive city. Nonetheless, if this plan comes to fruition the cut will be one of the coolest parts of the city.
